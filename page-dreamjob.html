My Dream Job

My dream job is to be a geneticist. Ever since I was in Kindergarten, I was always intrigued by cells and deoxyribonucleic acid and their functions. Because I'm still a primary school student, I could not learn about it in school, and learnt about it in a non-profit organization called Khan Academy and I learnt lot about DNA and RNA.

I love microbiology and chemistry, so being a geneticist seems about perfect for me. However, my parents say that to take up genetics, I have to take triple Science and Additional Mathematics. Although I know it will be hard, I will persevere and not give up. I also compiled a word document about everything I learnt in DNA. Go to File HERE

I want to be a geneticist because I read about it in a series of books. I learnt that geneticists did all kinds of experiments and I wanted to do so too! The series was called Run Hide Seek. It talks about a post-apocalyptic Singapore, when strange mutations occur in humans, causing them to become zombies. There was a team of rebel geneticists who try to stop all of them, by studying them and comparing their DNA with human DNA.
