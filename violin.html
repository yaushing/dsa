I AM ... A VIOLINIST

My Violin Journey

I've been playing the violin since I moved to Singapore from Hong Kong, after I heard my sister and my father playing violin in Hong Kong. The beautiful music enticed me to start learning.There were many times when I wanted to quit as it was hard, but my wonderful violin teacher helped me continue. My violin teacher is probably the best teacher anyone could have.

The one music artist that motivates me is Johann Sebastian Bach.I think that he is the greatest song composer. He wrote my favourite songs including Sinfonia in D major BWV 789 and Prelude in C major BWV 846. The one which icon is Bach Eternal is mainly prelude in C major,BWV 846.
